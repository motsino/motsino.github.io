Frequently asked questions

Below are some of the frequently asked questions by clients who wish to work with us.

  • 01

    Do you provide hosting services for websites?

    Oh yes! we do. We provide dependable web hosting services to interested clients at a great price.
    We have a free shared hosting plans for clients whose websites we've worked on and a Premium Shared Hosting Plan for other clients who just want to host their websites. Our premium plan lets you create up to 30 websites and host 5000 visitors. You'll also get a free domain and a free Let's Encrypt SSL certificate to secure your website.

  • 02

    Can I add a web scraping feature to my website?

    Certainly you can.
    We have various web scraping tools to help simplify the process of data extraction from websites. Some of the tools we use include; Scraper API, Scrapy, Octoparse and Beautiful Soup(Python web scraping library).

  • 03

    How long does it take for your websites to be ready for your clients?

    Well it all depends on the content, purpose and technicality of the website.
    A basic individual Portfolio website may take a day or two to be ready for execution.
    Creating a Portfolio site for companies require more detailing and build so give or take 4 - 5 days should be enough to accomplish the task.
    If it's going to be a business site e.g an E-commerce site, more time will be needed for advanced integrations that will enable transactions and other commercial attributes.
    One thing you should know is that we're steadfast and we take pains to satisfy our clients' criteria.

  • 04

    Is it possible to get free templates of your previous works?

    Assuredly yes, we do give out templates of our previously worked on projects. All you need do is send us a message via email here and we'll do our best to get them across to you.

  • 05

    How can we get copywriters for our websites?

    No worries, We have skilled and talented copywriters that can help boost your brands' awareness and attract target audience on what you do.
    Our copywriters offer their services at reasonable and affordable rates that are convenient for our clients and help to keep their worries at bay.
